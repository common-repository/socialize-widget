Socialize Widget

== Description ==

A nice and easy way to increase likes for your blog or website.
Add this widget to your site, adjust settings and you will have Facebook, Google, Twitter, Pinterest, StumbleUpon, LinkedIn Like buttons at the bottom of your page

== Installation ==

Upload the Socialize Widget plugin to your blog. Activate it, DONE!

1, 2, 3: You're done!
